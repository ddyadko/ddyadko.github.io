What is your justification to disclose personal details?

I truly believe having "skin in the game" (reducing anonymity) in regard to online discussions will increase the human aspect.

Why does this website look and feel bare-bone

I do not want to distract people from the message(s) I want to convey by implementing arbitraire cosmetics. Besides I truly believe we are over-stimulated in the Western world which is the cause of many psychological stressors we experience. Less stimulation feels more freeing. Expect a full blogpost about my ideas about this in the near future. Besides: it reduces maintainance, improves security and is mobile-responsive

Why did you make this website?

It started out with not being able to post my thoughts on LinkedIn due to characther limitations. This moved me to screenshotting my thoughts from a Word Processor and posting it. This made me realise that perhaps creatign a personal blog would be a better solution. Only time will tell.
